Dr Jiang's Quantum-Classical Computer-Aided Design Lab (JQub)

Lab Introduction:
JQub is established at George Mason University in Fall 2021. The Director of JQub is Dr. Weiwen Jiang, who was the Postdoctoral Associate in Prof. Yiyu Shi's team at the University of Notre Dame. He is the Ph.D. of Prof. Edwin H.-M. Sha at Chongqing University and co-advised by Prof. Jingtong Hu at the University of Pittsburgh, spending 2 years to conduct resarch at Pittsburgh in his Ph.D.

The team focuses on the hardware/software co-design for the broadly computing systems. The target application ranges from computer vision, natural language processing to medical-related artificial intelligence applications. The target hardware platform ranges from classical computing (i.e., FPGAs, ASICs, Computing-in-Memory, Mobile Phones, etc.) to quantum computing. The open projects from JQub can be accessed from GitHub.

The team is open for collaboration from both academic and industry. For contacting, please email to jqub.gmu@gmail.com.
